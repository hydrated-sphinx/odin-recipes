ice-cream

It's cold. Ice cold. It has cream. It's Ice-cream.

Ingredients

    Ice
  • Cream
  • Milk
  • Sorbet
  • Assorted Fruits of your choosing

Steps

  1. Chuck your ice-cream into a pot of boiling water
  2. Watch the bubbles bubble
  3. Cook your ice with some n-ice spices (here is where you throw in that one piece of basil. wait, you're saying you already used it? Huh, guess I need to rewrite this guide)
